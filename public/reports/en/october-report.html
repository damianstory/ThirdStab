Micro Grant Recipients

20 √ó $500

Twenty Canadian students received micro grants as a result of their mining career related exploration.

As part of this challenge, students were asked to:

  • Use MiHR resources to research one specific role in the industry
  • Explain what someone in that role is responsible for on a day to day, along with the skills required, and post-secondary pathways required to work in that role
  • Outline where in Canada these jobs are available, and a specific action the industry is taking towards sustainability initiatives
  • Take all of that research and include it in a 60-90 sec short form video
